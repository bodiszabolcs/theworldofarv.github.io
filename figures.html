Historical Figures

Elonias

Elonias was said to be the most powerful mortal in the history of Arv. His powers rivaled the gods' themselves. He united the kingdoms of Kramesh and led the fight against the Arch Dragons, released by Ond to destroy Earth. He was an expert in both magic and physical fighting.

It wasn't only his power, that was remarkable,though. His natural ability to bring people together almost seemed like a miracle. His charisma and wits were the reason he became King of Kramesh, shortly after the War Against the Dragons. He was kind-hearted, brave and loving.

While his life, before the war is a mystery, many tales have been written about it. Some tell he was born into a regular, low-class family, while others say he was made by the gods themselves, to rise up and defeat the Arch Dragons. Many other legends surround his name. It is believed he could slay a dragon with a single snap of his finger or that he gained an appearence with the 12 gods and fought Krieg ending in a draw.

He never fought alone however. His greatsword, Lubius, more commonly known as The Sky Splitter was always by his side. People believe it was sentient. That a soul lived in it and it wasn't a mere tool, but a great friend of Elonias.

Ivo Merkin

Ivo, Master of the Arcane was a powerful wizard, and a companion of Elonias. Ivo was a resident of Hiarum, an area near the Endless Forest, that was considered the most diverse and peaceful place in Kramesh, before it was eradicated by Rakash, the Thunderous. Ivo and his good friend, Upuc tried to defeat Rakash by themselves, but their powers weren't enough. Hiarum was almost completely eradicated, when Ivo had the idea to flee into the Endless Forest with the ramining people. Upuc held his ground against Rakash, while Ivo created a barrier around the Last Town of Hiarum, in the endless forest, hiding and protecing it from the dangers of the world.
